About Me

Hi there! My name is Nylma I am a fourth grade teacher who has been teaching for almost 10 years! During my time as a teacher I have specialized in Reading and Writing. In 2016, I was awarded Teacher of the Year and throughout the years I have accumulated anchor charts and other resources that have helped me in my teaching and allowed students to reach their full potential and really master concepts in reading and writing that have allowed them to excel in their work.

I am now a Reading and Writing Coach who specializes in helping teachers develop their reading and writing strategies. I love to be able to help develop teachers as I pass on tips and strategies I have learned over the years. I believe that with my background and training coupled with my years of experience I have accumulated, I am able to better equip and prepare teachers for implementing strong strategies for Reading and Writing in their classroom.

Please feel free to look through my charts and find one that can help you. If you have any questions about the charts please feel free to contact me. I hope these charts help you as much as they have helped me in my years.
